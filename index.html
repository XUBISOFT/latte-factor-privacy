Privacy Policy

Latte Factor – Savings Planner  ·  Last updated: May 18, 2025

Latte Factor ("the App") does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected by design.

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App — including habit names, amounts, birth year, and your name — is stored exclusively on your device using standard local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We have no servers or databases
  • We have no user accounts or login
  • We do not use analytics or crash tracking
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone

In-App Purchases

In-App Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We receive no payment information, card numbers, or personal data from Apple. The purchase record (Pro unlocked: yes/no) is stored locally on your device only.

Notifications

If you enable monthly reminders, the notification is scheduled locally on your device using iOS's built-in notification system. No data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. There is nothing to collect.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since we collect no data, material changes are unlikely.
